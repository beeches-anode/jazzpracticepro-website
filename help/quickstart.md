# JazzPracticePro Quickstart Guide

Get up and running in minutes with JazzPracticePro.

## First-Time Setup

### 1. Complete Onboarding

When you first open JazzPracticePro, you'll be guided through:
- **Welcome screen**: Learn what JazzPracticePro offers
- **Instrument selection**: Choose your primary instrument (this seeds relevant practice pillars)
- **Experience context**: Describe your playing level (helps AI provide personalized feedback)
- **Pillar preview**: See 5-7 recommended practice categories for your instrument
- **Authentication** (optional): Sign in with email + OTP code for cloud backup access
- **Restore options**: Import existing data or start fresh

**Note**: You can skip authentication and use the app entirely offline. Sign-in is only needed for cloud backup.

## Core Workflows

### 2. Log Your First Practice Session

**Quick Entry:**
1. Tap "Log Practice" from the home screen
2. Select your instrument and date
3. Add exercises to relevant practice pillars (e.g., "Repertoire", "Technique")
4. Each exercise includes a description and duration in minutes
5. Optionally add session notes
6. Save your session

**Fast Entry with Voice Import:**
1. Open the practice editor and tap "Voice Import"
2. Speak what you practiced: _"I did 20 minutes of long tones and 30 minutes on Giant Steps"_
3. Review the transcription (edit if needed)
4. Let AI parse your spoken notes into structured entries
5. Preview parsed entries and confirm
6. Entries merge into your session automatically (5-10x faster!)

### 3. Track Your Repertoire

1. Navigate to the Repertoire page
2. Search for a tune from our database of 525+ jazz standards (fuzzy search works great!)
3. Add tunes to your personal repertoire
4. When you practice a tune, reference it in your practice session
5. View practice history and momentum for each tune

### 4. Customize Your Practice Pillars

**What are practice pillars?**
Pillars are customizable practice categories that structure your sessions. You have 30 total slots:
- 2 system pillars (Repertoire, Transcription) - cannot be renamed
- 28 custom pillars - activate from templates or create your own

**To customize:**
1. Go to Settings → Practice Settings
2. Choose from 21 pedagogically-grounded templates (recommended based on your instrument)
3. Rename pillars to match your teacher's terminology
4. Configure which pillars appear for each instrument (great for multi-instrumentalists!)
5. Reset unused pillars to free up slots (only works if no practice data exists)

### 5. Backup Your Data

**Local Backup (no sign-in required):**
1. Go to Settings → Local Backup
2. Tap "Export Backup"
3. Download JSON file to your device
4. Store safely for later import

**Cloud Backup (requires sign-in):**
1. Sign in with email (you'll receive a 6-digit OTP code)
2. Go to Settings → Cloud Backup
3. Create a passphrase (12+ characters, never sent to server!)
4. Tap "Create Cloud Backup"
5. Your data is encrypted on your device, then uploaded to secure cloud storage
6. Restore anytime with your email and passphrase

### 6. Get AI-Powered Feedback

**Prerequisites:** Sign in to check AI credit balance (free tier includes 5 AI calls/month)

**To generate feedback:**
1. Navigate to AI Feedback page
2. Choose feedback type (weekly review, practice plan, etc.)
3. Tap "Generate AI Feedback"
4. AI analyzes your recent practice patterns
5. Get personalized insights, strengths, and actionable suggestions
6. Credits are automatically reserved, confirmed on success, or refunded on failure

## Pro Tips

- **Voice Import**: Use voice import to log practice hands-free while warming down or packing up
- **Session Notes**: Add context to sessions - AI uses these for more personalized feedback
- **Tune History**: Update repertoire after each focused session to see progress over time
- **Pillar Visibility**: Multi-instrumentalists can configure different pillars for each instrument
- **Statistics**: Check the Statistics page regularly to track streaks and identify practice patterns
- **Backup Regularly**: Create cloud or local backups weekly to protect your practice data
