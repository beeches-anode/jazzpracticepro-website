Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with JazzPracticePro. Our refund policy is as follows:

When Refunds Are Available

We offer refunds where required under Australian Consumer Law. Refunds may be granted in cases such as:

  • Billing errors: You were charged in error (e.g., duplicate charges, charged after cancellation)
  • Technical issues: The service is not functioning as described and we cannot resolve the issue
  • Unauthorized charges: Charges were made without your authorization
  • First-time subscribers: Within 14 days of your first subscription payment if you are not satisfied with the service

How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund:

  1. Contact us at contact@jazzpracticepro.com
  2. Include your account email address and reason for the refund request
  3. Submit your request within 14 days of the charge
  4. We will review your request and respond within 3-5 business days

Note: Refund requests submitted more than 14 days after the charge may be denied unless there are exceptional circumstances.

Refund Processing

If your refund is approved:

  • Refunds are processed through Stripe, our payment processor
  • Refunds typically appear in your account within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank
  • You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed
  • Your subscription will be cancelled immediately upon refund

When Refunds Are Not Available

Refunds are generally not provided for:

  • Change of mind after using the service beyond the 14-day period
  • Partial refunds for unused portions of monthly or annual subscriptions
  • AI credits that have already been used
  • Subscriptions cancelled mid-billing period (access continues until period end)

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no cancellation fees or penalties.

How to Cancel

You have two options to cancel your JazzPracticePro subscription:

Option 1: Cancel Through the App

  1. Log in to your JazzPracticePro account
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Subscription
  3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Confirm your cancellation

Option 2: Cancel by Email

Send an email to contact@jazzpracticepro.com with:

  • Subject line: "Cancel Subscription"
  • Your account email address
  • We will process your cancellation within 1 business day

What Happens When You Cancel

  • ✓ Continued access: You will keep access to JazzPracticePro premium features until the end of your current billing period.
  • ✓ No future charges: You will not be charged for any future billing periods.
  • ✓ Data retention: Your practice data remains accessible in the free tier of the app.
  • ✓ Resubscribe anytime: You can reactivate your subscription at any time in the future.

Cancellation Timing

To avoid being charged for the next billing period:

  • Monthly subscriptions: Cancel at least 24 hours before your next billing date
  • Annual subscriptions: Cancel at least 7 days before your renewal date

Tip: You can check your next billing date in your account settings under Subscription details.

Free Tier After Cancellation

When your premium subscription ends, your account automatically reverts to the free tier, which includes:

  • Full practice tracking and logging
  • Repertoire management
  • Basic statistics
  • Limited AI credits (monthly allocation)
  • All your existing practice data

You can continue using JazzPracticePro for free indefinitely, with access to all core features.

Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this refund policy limits your rights under the Australian Consumer Law (ACL). Under the ACL, you are entitled to:

  • Services that are provided with acceptable care and skill
  • Services that are fit for the purpose we said they would be fit for
  • Services delivered within a reasonable time

If we fail to meet these guarantees, you may be entitled to a remedy under the ACL.
