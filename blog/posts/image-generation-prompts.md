## General Tips for Modern AI Image Generators (2025)

### Aspect Ratios

- **Hero image:** 16:9 or 2:1 ratio
- **Blog header:** 2:1 or 3:1 ratio
- **Featured image:** 1.91:1 (Facebook/LinkedIn standard)

### Negative Prompts (add these if your tool supports them)

```
cluttered, messy, unprofessional, text, words, letters, typography, watermark, signature, low quality, blurry, distorted
```

### Style Modifiers to Consider

- Add "high quality, detailed, professional" to any prompt
- For more realistic: "photorealistic, professional photography"
- For more graphic: "vector illustration, clean lines, modern design"
- For softer look: "soft lighting, warm tones, inviting"

---

## Color Palette Reference

- **Primary Purple:** #7c3aed (matches JazzPracticePro brand)
- **Secondary Blue:** #3b82f6
- **Background:** White or light gray (#f9fafb)
- **Accents:** Warm grays for depth

## Usage Notes

- **Option 1** is recommended for the main hero image - it's conceptual, professional, and clearly represents the "organized practice" theme
- **Option 3** works well if you want something more educational/infographic style
- **Option 2** is good if you prefer a more literal, relatable scene
- All prompts explicitly avoid text to prevent unwanted words in generated images
- Adjust aspect ratios based on where the image will be used (hero vs. featured image)
- For 2025 AI generators, prompts are more effective when specific about colors (hex codes) and style
- Consider generating multiple variations and selecting the best match for your blog's aesthetic
