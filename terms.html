Service Description

JazzPracticePro is a practice journal and educational support tool designed for jazz musicians. The service provides:

  • Practice session tracking and logging
  • Repertoire management with spaced repetition
  • Optional AI-powered practice feedback and suggestions
  • Statistics and progress tracking
  • Local-first data storage with optional cloud backup

JazzPracticePro is provided for educational and practice support purposes. It is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
